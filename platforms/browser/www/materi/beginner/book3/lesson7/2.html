Grammar: Preposition of Time: in and on

Thomas Alfa Edision was born In 1847
In February
On February 11
On February 11, 1847

NOTE:

>> Use in for years and months

>> Use on for days and dates.

>> Don't use an article with years, mnths, days or dates.

Dates are writen like this:
February 11 , 1847
(month) (day) (comma) (year)
