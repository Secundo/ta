Grammar: some and any

There are two kinds of nouns in English.

COUNTBLE NOUNS UNCOUNTABLE NOUNS
A. Banana A. Ice Cream
B. Apples B. Lettuce
NOTE: Countable nouns are singular and plural. Use a or an with singular countable nouns. NOTE: Uncountable nouns are singular. Don't use a or an with uncountable nouns.
