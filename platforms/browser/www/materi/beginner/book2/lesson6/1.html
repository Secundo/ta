Grammar: Affirmative Statements in the Present Continous

I'm talking to a woman.
He's talking to a woman.
She's talking to a woman.
We're talking to a woman.
They're talking to a woman.

NOTE:

Present continous
= am/is/are + verb + -ing

Example: Youre talking.

The present continous tense is used for things that are happening now.
