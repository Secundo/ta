From The Video : Intonation

Statement Intonation      WH-Question Intonation      Yes/No Question Intonation
Maybe he went home.      Whose is it?      Is anyone here?
 
 
NOTE :
Rising intonation is used at the end of yes/no question; falling intonation is used at the end
of statements and WH-questions. (WH-questions use the following words : Who, Whom,
Whose What, Which, When, Where, Why, and How.)
