Grammar : The Possesive Form of Nouns

Amy's sister's name is Marilyn.
Charles's last name is Sutton.
Billy and Johnny are boy's names. 		NOTE :
Add 's to nouns to show ownership or
possession. You can add 's or '   to singular
words that end in s. Both forms are correct.
Add '   to plural nouns that end in s.
