NOTE :
Use Mr. or Ms. + a last name in bussines settings or for
people you don't know very well.
Miss, madam and sir can be used without a name as a form
of addres. They are commonly used for people you don't know.
Lady is not used as a form of address.
Mister is used most often with expression of anger or irritation.
