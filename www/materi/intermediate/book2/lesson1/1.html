Grammar : a and some

COUNTABLE NOUNS
 
a sweater   some sweaters
    some pajamas
UNCOUNTABLE NOUNS
 
 
some time
NOTE :
Use a with singular countable nouns;
use some with plural nouns and uncountable nouns.
Nouns for things with two parts, like sunglasses, pants, and pajamas, are
always plural.
