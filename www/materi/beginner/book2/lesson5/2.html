Grammar: Questions withwhere, what, how, why and who

1. Why do you have a headache?
Because I have to work with Jake.

2. How are you?
I'm great. I'm happy.

3. Whereis the job?
in Denver. KDEN, Denver.

4. Who's this, Rita?
My friend Chris.

5. Howtall is she?
She's tall-about five eight.

6. What's the matter?
I have a cold. My whole body aches.

7. What does she do?
She teaches sailing.
