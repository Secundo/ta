Grammar: Prepsition of Time

at 6:00 on Monday(s) in the morning
at 12:00 (noon) on Tuesday(s) in the afternoon
at 12:00 on the weekend in the evening
at night

NOTE:

on Monday = this Monday, last Monday, or every Monday.

on Mondays = every Monday

in the morning BUT on Monday mornings

at night BUT on Monday nights
