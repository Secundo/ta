Grammar: Yes/No Questions with going to

Is John going to move to Denver?
Are John and Susan going to move to Denver?
NOTE: Today is May 7. Next week is May 14. Next month is June.

Is Susan going to move to Denver? Yes, she is.
No, she isn't.

Are John and Susan going to get married? Yes, they are.
No, they aren't.
NOTE: Yes/No questions can also be answered with only Yes or No.
