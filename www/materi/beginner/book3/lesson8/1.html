Spelling: Regular Verbs in the Past Tense

D Ed Ied
Decide + d Watch+ ed Study + ied
Smoke + d Play + ed

NOTE:

>> Add d to verbs ending in e.

>> Add ed to verbs ending in consonant.

>> Add ed to verbs ending in a vowel + y.

>> Drop the y and ied to verbs ending in consonant + y.

>> The ending ed after a/t/or/d sound is pronounced as an separate syllable: visit .ed = /id/.
