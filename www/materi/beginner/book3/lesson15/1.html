Grammar: Questions with when, where, and what time

Where's he going to go? To Denver.
When's he going to leave? On June 17th.
What time is he going to leave? At 3:00.

NOTE:

Where's = where is

When's = when is

For the example:

Where's John going to go? He's going to go to Denver.
When's she going to leave? She's going leave on July 1st.
