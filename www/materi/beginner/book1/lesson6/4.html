Grammar: Short Answers

A good cook? Yes, you are. No, you aren't.
A good cook? Yes, I am. No, I'm not.
A good cook? Yes, he is. No, he isn't.
A good cook? Yes, she is. No, she isn't.
Good cook? Yes, they are. No, they aren't.

NOTE: you can also say: No, you're not. No, he's not. No, they're not.
