Spelling: Noun Plurals

SINGULAR
PLURAL
An egg Eggs
A cook Cooks
NOTE: Add s to form the plural. Don't use a or an with plural words.
An actress Actresses
A sandwich Sandwiches
NOTE: Add es to wrds ending in s,sh,ch,x, or z. The ending es after s, sh,ch,x, or z is pronounced as a separate syllable:/lz/atress.es
A country Countries
A boy Boys
A day Days
NOTE: Drop the y and add ies to words ending in y, unless a vowel (a,e,i,o,u) is before y.
