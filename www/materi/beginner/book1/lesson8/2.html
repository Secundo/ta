Useful Every Phrases

Susan: You're a good newscaster.

John: Thank you.

Linda: Good job, Jake.

Jake: Thanks.

Jake: Congratulations, Webster.

Susan: Thank you.

Rita: Good luck.

Susan: Thanks.

Carol: Coffee?

Susan: Yes, please.

John: Ciggarette?

Susan: No, thanks.

NOTE: Thanks = Thank you (but it less formal).
