Spelling: The -s Forms of Verbs

-s
Read- reads
Work-works
Live-lives
NOTE: Add s to the simple form of the verb. The -s form of have is has.

-es
Watch-watches
Fix-fixes
Do-does
Go-goes
NOTE: Add es to verbs .ending in s, ch, x, or z, Add es to do and go. The ending es after s, ch, sh, x or z is pronounced as separate. Syllable/lz/:watch-es.

-ies
Study-studies
Play-plays
NOTE: Drop the y and add ies to words ending in y, unless a vowel (a,e,i,o,u) is beforoe y.
